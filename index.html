About Us

New Horizon Scholar School, Thane West is dedicated to providing a holistic education that nurtures the intellectual, emotional, and social development of our students.

Our mission is to foster a learning environment that encourages curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking.

Vision: To empower students to become lifelong learners and responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Mission: To provide quality education through innovative teaching methods.

Leadership Team

Principal: Mrs. Simmi Juneja
With 30 years of experience in education, she leads the school with a vision for academic excellence and student development. She fosters a positive school culture and encourages collaboration among staff, students, and parents.

Vice Principal: Mrs. Sharmila Kulkarni
She assists the Principal in day-to-day administration and plays a key role in implementing educational programs that meet the diverse needs of our students.

Head of Academic Affairs: Mrs. Sudha Rao
Responsible for curriculum development and academic standards, she ensures that the school maintains high educational quality and supports teachers in delivering effective instruction.

Academics

At New Horizon Scholar School, we are committed to providing a comprehensive academic experience that empowers students to reach their full potential.

Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking, creativity,and problem-solving skills, offering a balanced approach that integrates academic learning with practical application.Follows the CBSE syllabus, blending academics with extracurricular activities.

Grade Levels and Subjects Offered

Pre-Primary: Nursery, LKG, UKG
Primary: Classes 1 to 5
Middle: Classes 6 to 8
Secondary: Classes 9 to 10
Senior Secondary: Classes 11 to 12 (Science, Commerce, Arts)

Languages: English, Hindi, and a regional language.
Core subjects: Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), Social Studies, Arts & Crafts, Physical Education, and Computer Science.

Learning Support

We provide tailored support for students with learning differences and offer after-school tutoring to ensure that every student can succeed.

Classrooms are equipped with smart boards and access to online learning platforms to enhance interactive learning.

Extracurricular Programs

We encourage students to explore their interests through various clubs and sports programs that promote teamwork and physical fitness.

Parent and Community Engagement

We actively involve parents through workshops and collaborate with community organizations to provide real-world learning experiences.
